Zan Gojcic

I am a senior research scientist at NVIDIA working on 3D deep learning in the group of Sanja Fidler. I have received my PhD from ETH Zurich in 2021 where I worked with Andreas Wieser and Konrad Schindler. During my PhD I have also visited the geometric computation group led by Leonidas Guibas at Stanford University. My research interests are focused around 3D deep learning, weakly supervised learning, and domain adaptation. Outside of research I enjoy travelling, playing soccer, cooking, and spending quality time with my friends.
